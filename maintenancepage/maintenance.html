Why is Maintenance Crucial?

Many assume rain is enough to clean solar panels. In reality, rain often leaves dirty residue. Without professional care, you face:

  • Financial Loss: Reduced energy production means higher electricity bills.
  • Safety Hazards: Dust buildup can create "Hotspots" that risk fire.
  • Warranty Void: Manufacturers often require proof of maintenance.
